Community Powered Aviation

The Sport Pilot Study Bank is a collaborative club project born out of the QueLab Makerspace and the Sport Flying Club of New Mexico.

Our mission aligns with the open-access philosophy of HamStudy.org: providing accessible, high-quality learning modules to help individuals achieve their certifications and safe flight goals.

  • Aligned with current FAA Airman Certification Standards
  • Completely Free & Open Source
  • Community maintained question pools
